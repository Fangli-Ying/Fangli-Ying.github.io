About me

I received the B.S. degree from the Zhejiang University of Science and Technology, in 2009, and the Ph.D. degree from Maynooth University, in 2013. I am currently a Lectuer of Computer Science in East China University of Science and Technology in Shanghai China. My main research interests include Computer Vision, Few-shot Learning and Computational Biology. Also, I do some research about Time Series Prediction, such as Financial Trading. I focus on using AI to solve practical problems.
